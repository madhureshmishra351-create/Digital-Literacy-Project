# Staying Safe Online Is Important.

We stay on the internet all the time while we study online, scroll through media, chat with friends, and when we pay our bills online. The internet has made our life very easy. It has also brought some problems. Cybercrime is happening more and more. College students are the common target because they have greater online presence . They do not know what to be careful about. The good thing is that it is easy to stay safe. You just need to do things every day to stay safe online.

**Things To Keep You Safe Online**

* You should use a password for every account. Do not use the password for everything that is a big mistake.

* Do not click on any links that come in your emails or messages or social media. Even if they look real they might not be real.

* Download files from reputed sources. If something doesn't seem right it probably isn't.

* You should have two-factor authentication whenever you can. That extra step can stop someone from getting into your account.

* You should keep your phone and computer and apps updated all the time. Those updates are important for a reason so keep them updated.

* You should be careful when you are using Wi-Fi. Do not check your bank account or do anything when you are connected to Wi-Fi it is not safe.

Things To Keep Your Money Safe

* Never tell anyone your code or your UPI PIN or any of your banking information. No one from the bank will ever ask you for these things so do not give them out.

* Before you send money to someone make sure you are sending it to the person. One wrong tap can cost you a lot of money so be careful, with money transactions.

What To Do If Someone Tricks You

If you or someone you know has been tricked, stay calm, and call the police right away. You can go to the National Cyber Crime Portal(https://cybercrime.gov.in.), You can also call the Cybercrime Helpline(1930); they help people who have lost money because of cybercrime. If you act fast you might be able to get your money back. Staying safe online. Keeping your money safe is very important. Always remember to stay safe and keep your money safe.
