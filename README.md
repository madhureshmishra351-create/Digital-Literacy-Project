#  Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist for College Students

##  Do’s
-  Think before posting anything online because it represents your personality and image.  
-  Use respectful and polite language while communicating with others on social media.  
-  Verify the information before sharing it to make sure it is true and reliable.  
-  Use social media platforms for learning, networking, and sharing useful knowledge.  
-  Keep your personal information private and use strong privacy settings.  

---

##  Don’ts
-  Do not post offensive, abusive, or disrespectful comments about others.  
-  Avoid sharing fake news or information that you are not sure about.  
-  Do not reveal personal details like passwords, phone numbers, or home address online.  
-  Avoid spending too much time on social media that affects your studies or productivity.  
-  Do not upload content that may harm your reputation or create problems in the future.
